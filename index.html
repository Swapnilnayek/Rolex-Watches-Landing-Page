Overview

Green aventurine dial- This dial features hour markers in 18 ct gold, set with 32 diamonds. Also Roman VI and IX in 18 ct gold, set with 24 diamonds.

18 ct Everose gold- To preserve the beauty of its pink gold watches, Rolex created and patented an exclusive 18 ct pink gold alloy cast in its own foundry: Everose gold.

The President bracelet- It represents the ultimate in refinement and comfort and is always made of carefully selected precious metals.
